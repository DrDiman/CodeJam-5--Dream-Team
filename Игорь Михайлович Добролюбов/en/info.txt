Born on October 22, 1933 in the city of Novosibirsk.

In 1956 he graduated from the Faculty of Journalism of the Belarusian State University.

In 1963 - VGIK (workshop of Mikhail Romm).

In 1956-1958 - actor of the Belarusian Republican Youth Theater.

Since 1963 - director of the film studio "Belarusfilm".

From 1974 to 1975 - Secretary of the Union of Cinematographers of the BSSR [2].

Since 1993 - Professor of the Institute of Modern Knowledge named after A. Shirokov (Minsk).

He died on July 19, 2010 in Minsk. He was buried on July 22, 2010 at the Eastern Cemetery in Minsk.
